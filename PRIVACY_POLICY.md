## What types of information do we collect?

BookSuggestr collects the following information to provide personalized book recommendations:

**Information you provide:**
- Book preferences (genres, book length, similar books, additional preferences)
- Reading history (books you've read, including title, author, and your rating, if you choose to import your reading history via CSV)
- Feedback on recommendations (like/dislike with optional comments)

**Information stored locally on your device:**
- All your reading history and preferences are stored locally in an encrypted database on your device
- Your recommendation history and feedback
- Your remaining suggestion credits

We do **not** collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics. We do **not** use any analytics SDKs.

## How we use your information

Your information is used solely to generate personalized book recommendations. When you request a recommendation:

- Your book preferences, reading history, and feedback are sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalized recommendations
- Book titles and authors are sent to Google Books API to retrieve book cover images

## Third-party services

The app uses the following third-party services:

### OpenAI
We use OpenAI's API to generate personalized book recommendations. When you request a recommendation, the following data is sent to OpenAI:
- Your genre preferences
- Your book length preferences
- Similar book preferences
- Additional preferences you provide
- Your reading history (book titles, authors, and ratings)
- Your feedback on previous recommendations

Link to OpenAI's privacy policy: [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy/)

### Google Books API
We use Google Books API to retrieve book cover images. Only the book title and author name are sent to this service.

Link to Google's privacy policy: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### RevenueCat
We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases for suggestion credit packs. RevenueCat handles payment processing and may collect purchase-related information.

Link to RevenueCat's privacy policy: [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/)

## Data Storage
